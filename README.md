# Ship Bakes

Website for Ship Bakes — a home bakery specialising in birthday cakes, celebration cakes, and cupcakes. Built as a Mother's Day gift by her son.

**Built by [CronKode](https://github.com/CronKode)**

---

## Stack

- Plain HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks
- Hosted on Vercel
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Dancing Script, Lora, DM Sans)

## Photos needed

Replace the placeholder images in `index.html` with real photos:

| Location | Variable to replace | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hero background | `hero-bg.jpg` | Top-down cake shot, natural light, white surface |
| About section | `baker-photo.jpg` | Baker in kitchen, warm and authentic |
| Menu background | `menu-bg.jpg` | Any beautiful cake, slightly out of focus is fine |

**Photo tips for the best result:**
- Natural light (near a window, not flash)
- White or clean wooden surface
- Top-down angle for the cake shots
- Good focus on the cake, let the background go slightly soft
