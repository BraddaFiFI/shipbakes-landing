Meet Ship

The baker.
The artist.
The heart behind it all.

My name is Ship, and I've been turning flour, butter, and love into edible art for as long as I can remember. What started as a way to celebrate the people around me grew into something I couldn't stop even if I tried.

Every cake you see was made in my home kitchen, by my hands, with real ingredients and no shortcuts. I taste everything. I fix everything. I don't stop until it's right.

Your celebration deserves a cake that looks as special as it feels. That's why I'm here.
